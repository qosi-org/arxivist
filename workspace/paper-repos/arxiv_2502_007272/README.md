## What this paper does (plain English)

GENERator is a **generative** genomic foundation model: a LLaMA decoder-only transformer trained
with an autoregressive next-token objective on **386 billion nucleotides** of eukaryotic DNA, with a
context of up to **98,000 nucleotides**. Its key design choice is **6-mer tokenization** (rather than
single-nucleotide or BPE), which the paper shows is best for *autoregressive* genomic modeling. It
matches or beats Evo2 on sequence recovery while being ~19тАУ44├Ч faster, does alignment-free zero-shot
variant effect prediction, and тАФ via prompt-guided generation тАФ designs cis-regulatory elements whose
activity exceeds natural sequences (wet-lab validated).

This repo **fine-tunes the official pretrained GENERator** (`GenerTeam/GENERator-eukaryote-1.2b-base`,
a LLaMA causal LM: 26 layers, d 2048, 32 heads / 4 KV, vocab 4128, ctx 16384, SiLU тАФ all confirmed
against the HF config) on genomic classification benchmarks, and includes a zero-shot VEP path.

## тЪая╕П Hardware

GENERator-1B is **1.2B parameters (~5 GB bf16)**. Full fine-tuning needs an **A100-class GPU** тАФ it
will OOM on a 16 GB T4. For tighter GPUs, set `model.load_in_8bit: true` in the config (needs
`bitsandbytes`) and reduce `batch_size`. The reproduction targets the paper's actual A100 setup.

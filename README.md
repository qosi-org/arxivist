## What this paper does (plain English)

HyenaDNA is a genomic foundation model that reads DNA one nucleotide at a time and can handle
sequences up to **1 million tokens** — roughly 500× longer than attention-based genomic models —
by replacing self-attention with the **Hyena operator** (implicit long convolutions evaluated by
FFT, sub-quadratic in sequence length). Pretrained on the human reference genome (hg38) with a
next-token objective, it fine-tunes to state-of-the-art on GenomicBenchmarks (7/8) and Nucleotide
Transformer (12/18) tasks while using far fewer parameters than baseline Transformers.

This repo **fine-tunes official pretrained HyenaDNA weights** (from HuggingFace) on genomic
classification tasks. A from-scratch reference Hyena operator is included (`models/hyena_operator.py`)
for study, but the reproduction path uses the released checkpoints.

## Reproducibility notes

This reproduction is faithful to what the paper states; several details were **inferred** and are
flagged here (from the SIR confidence annotations):

- **Training hyperparameters (SIR conf 0.62):** LR `6e-5` (downstream), cosine schedule, 10% warmup,
  `bf16`, grad-clip `1.0` — all marked `# ASSUMED` in [configs/config.yaml](configs/config.yaml).
  The paper reports ranges (LR 1.5e-4–6e-4 pretrain) that vary by model size.
- **Hyena filter internals (SIR conf 0.55):** the implicit-filter MLP in the *from-scratch* operator
  follows the Hyena reference (Poli et al. 2023), not this paper. It will **not** match the released
  checkpoints numerically — hence the pretrained-weights path is the default.
- **Pre-norm residuals & weight-tied head (conf 0.65–0.7):** standard-practice assumptions.
- **Pooling:** mean-pool over the sequence for classification (`# ASSUMED`; alt: last-token).

Known deviations: exact per-variant batch/LR schedules and sequence-length warmup are simplified.
